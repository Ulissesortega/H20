2nd Tip: How to correctly care for your lawn
  • Water early in the morning before 9 a.m. or after 5 p.m.
  • Water your lawn just 2 to 3 times a week.
  • Water only when your lawn needs it.
  • Water your lawn on calm days. You'll avoid losing sprinkling water by strong winds if you do
  • When cutting the lawn, set the mower blade to 2-3 inches high.
  • Keep your mower blades sharp
